WHY SOLIS?

American Made

All Solis cars are designed and manufactured using 100% American labor and using sustainably sourced materials from countries given higher than an 7/10 on the Human Freedom Index. We want our customers to feel that they are truly making a difference, not just for the environment, but also for the human condition.
